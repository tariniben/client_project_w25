First Generation Events

Winter Community Dinner

31st January 2024, 5:30-7:30pm @ Michigan League Ballroom

This event is an opportunity for first-generation college students to network with each other. There will be a resource at this event that is dedicated to supporting first-generation students and staff will be available to answer your questions.

Register for the event on the Student Life Website.

First-Gen Day of Visibility

3rd November 2024, 12:00-1:30pm @ Mason Hall Posting Wall

Join us for the end of First-Generation College Student Celebration Week by taking part in an interactive photo event with CAPS! We will be wearing our First-Generation apparel and we hope you do too!

Register for the event on the Student Life Website.

First-Gen Mentor Meet

2nd November 2024, 5:30-7:30pm @ Student Activities Building Atrium

Join the First-Gen Gateway for snacks, games, and a “speed mentoring” event! We have assembled a cohort of first-generation graduate students, law students, and PHD students for small-group discussion and socializing.

Register for the event on the Student Life Website.
