Academic Planning

Degree Requirements

An overview of the BSI degree structure to aid your planning process.

Your Time Before UMSI: 55-60+ Credits

This includes all the transfer credit you have from high school e.g. AP, IB, and A-Levels as well as all your credits from the University of Michigan.

Your Time at UMSI: 60 Credits

This includes a minimum of 45 SI Upper Level credits, consisting of required and elective coursework. The remaining 15 credits can be SI or not, as long as they are 300+ level courses.

Academic Advisors

Academic advising is a process where students plan, execute, and reflect on their academic plans with guidance from a UMSI Academic Advisor.

What can my advisor help me with?

  • Creating an individualized academic plan
  • Interpreting an academic policy or procedure
  • Discussing concerns about a particular course
  • Connecting with student support resources and services
  • Navigating a disruption to your BSI experience

How can I get the most value out of my appointments?

  1. Identify the appropriate service: scheduled appointments, drop-in appointments, or email
  2. Be aware of important deadlines and reach out well in advance
  3. Prepare for appointments by reflecting on your goals and creating a list of questions
  4. Follow through and follow-up with your advisor
