CAPS

Services Offered

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides a range of nationally credited clinical, preventative, and training services.


Clinical Services

This is a collaborative process between a client and counselor that begins with establishing a trusting and respectful relationship in a caring and nonjudgmental environment.

Crisis Services

This service is available for students needing immediate, same-day access to a mental health professional during our business hours. It can be used for reasons such as not feeling safe, experiencing a sudden loss, etc.

Consultation

This involves early identification of concerns and appropriate intervention to better assist connecting students, faculty, staff, etc. with needed services.

Care Management

This branch connects students with resources both on and off campus. It also connects them with providers across campus and in the Ann Arbor community.
